FAQ

Do I need a special WhatsApp account?

You need a free Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API number (not your personal WhatsApp). Setup takes ~10 minutes — full steps are inside the app's Settings page.

Can I message anyone?

You can freely reply to anyone who messaged you in the last 24 hours. To start conversations (campaigns/drips), WhatsApp requires pre-approved message templates and opted-in contacts. The app enforces this so your number stays safe.

How does the AI responder work?

You paste your business info (products, prices, policies) once. Claude AI reads every inbound message and replies instantly in the customer's language, collects their name and interest, and saves them as a lead.

What does WhatsApp/Meta charge?

Meta's free tier covers 1,000 service conversations per month per number. Beyond that Meta bills your Meta account directly at their local rates — we never touch it.

Is my data safe?

Your contacts and conversations are stored per-account, isolated, and never shared or sold. Your WhatsApp token is stored encrypted at rest and only used to send your messages.

What happens after the 24h demo?

The demo account expires and locks. Create a real account anytime — imports take minutes.

🔌 WhatsApp connection

🧭 Guided setup — 10 minutes, once

1
Create your Meta app
Go to developers.facebook.com/apps → Create App → type Business → after creating, click Add product → WhatsApp → Set up.
2
Copy your Phone Number ID
In WhatsApp → API Setup you'll see "Phone number ID" under your number. Copy it into the field above.
3
Create a permanent token
Business Settings → System Users → Add → name it "wamarketer" (Admin) → Generate token → select your app → check whatsapp_business_messaging + whatsapp_business_management → copy the token above. (Tokens from API Setup expire in 24h — use a System User token!)
4
Connect the webhook
WhatsApp → Configuration → Webhook → Edit:
Callback URL: …
Verify token: wamark-verify
Then click Manage → subscribe to messages.
5
Save above → Send test message
If the test arrives on your phone — you're live. 🎉
