FAQ

Do I need a special WhatsApp account?

You connect a business WhatsApp number two ways: Meta WhatsApp Cloud API (free, ~10-min setup) or Twilio (fast start, pay-per-message). Either way it's a business number, not your personal WhatsApp. Full guided steps are in the app's Settings page.

Can I message anyone?

You can freely reply to anyone who messaged you in the last 24 hours. To start conversations (campaigns/drips), WhatsApp requires pre-approved message templates and opted-in contacts. The app enforces this so your number stays safe.

How does the AI responder work?

You paste your business info (products, prices, policies) once. Claude AI reads every inbound message and replies instantly in the customer's language, collects their name and interest, and saves them as a lead.

What does WhatsApp/Meta charge?

On Meta, the free tier covers 1,000 service conversations/month per number; beyond that Meta bills you directly. On Twilio, you pay Twilio per message. Either way the provider bills you directly — Z24SEVEN never touches those fees.

Is my data safe?

Your contacts and conversations are stored per-account, isolated, and never shared or sold. Your WhatsApp token is stored encrypted at rest and only used to send your messages.

How do I try it?

Book a free 30-minute live demo from the homepage — we set it up on your business and answer everything. Then we create your account and you're running in minutes.

Settings

Connect WhatsApp via Meta Cloud API (free) or Twilio (fast start) — your choice, ~10 minutes.

🔗 Choose your WhatsApp provider

Two ways to connect. Meta is free but needs a Facebook Business setup. Twilio is faster to start (works without a Meta developer account) but charges per message.

⚡ Twilio WhatsApp connection

Twilio setup (10 min): 1) Sign up at twilio.com. 2) Console → copy your Account SID + Auth Token. 3) Messaging → Try it out → WhatsApp sandbox (instant) or request a WhatsApp sender (production). 4) Copy the sandbox/sender number as whatsapp:+1... into "From". 5) In Twilio's WhatsApp Sandbox settings, set "When a message comes in" to: https://z24seven.tel/api/webhook (method POST).

🔌 WhatsApp connection (Meta)

🧭 Guided setup — 10 minutes, once

1
Create your Meta app
Go to developers.facebook.com/apps → Create App → type Business → after creating, click Add product → WhatsApp → Set up.
2
Copy your Phone Number ID
In WhatsApp → API Setup you'll see "Phone number ID" under your number. Copy it into the field above.
3
Create a permanent token
Business Settings → System Users → Add → name it "z24seven" (Admin) → Generate token → select your app → check whatsapp_business_messaging + whatsapp_business_management → copy the token above. (Tokens from API Setup expire in 24h — use a System User token!)
4
Connect the webhook
WhatsApp → Configuration → Webhook → Edit:
Callback URL: …
Verify token: wamark-verify
Then click Manage → subscribe to messages.
5
Save above → Send test message
If the test arrives on your phone — you're live. 🎉
